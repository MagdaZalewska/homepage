About me

Hi! My name is Magda.

So far, I have worked in companies with various business profiles, implementing projects as part of project teams, as a team leader and a member of the project team.

I cooperated with substantive departments and external suppliers of IT tools in the field of requirements analysis, modeling functionality, transparency and ease of use (e.g.: business e-portals for clients, SAP systems, sales systems), creating data migration procedures, testing adopted solutions and conducting training for end users.

The tasks performed concerned the implementation of projects in the following areas: legal, tax and accounting, compliance, sales and marketing.

Why did I sign up for the Frontend Developer course?

My previous professional experience and achievements motivated me to change the profile of my profession and focusing on development in the IT field. I decided to learn something new that would bring me satisfaction and what the results of the work will be visible immediately and will have a real effect.

I want to create websites and applications and implement systems that are visually attractive to users, a by therefore easy, friendly and quick to use.

That's why I decided to broaden my knowledge on the Frontend Developer course!
